About Our Club

RU Origami Club is built around the art of origami. We believe in sharing our knowledge and using it to give back in every way that we can, and hope to use our passion for origami to do more good in the world.

Weekly Meetings

We meet every Thursday at 8-9 PM in The Hatchery at Alexander Library.
